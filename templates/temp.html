Aluworks Limited Summary

Profile

Aluworks Limited is an aluminum continuous casting and cold rolling mill located in Tema, Ghana. The company was incorporated as a private company limited by shares on February 24, 1978, and granted a certificate to commence business on March 22, 1978. Aluworks's business is to produce and sell semi-finished flat-rolled sheet products for the building, construction, and household industries in Ghana and West Africa. The manufacturing company's principal products include (colored, plain, mill-finished or stucco-embossed, roller or tension-leveled) aluminum sheets-in-coil, slit coils and flat sheets, aluminum discs, plus roll-formed or corrugated sheets, ridges and louver blades. Aluworks was converted to a public limited liability company at its 9th annual general meeting on May 28, 1996. After an IPO in October 1996, the company was listed on the Ghana Stock Exchange on November 29, 1996 at the price of GHS 0.135 per share.
